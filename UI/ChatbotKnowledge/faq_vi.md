# FAQ FEvents

## Chinh sach ve
- Ve da mua khong hoan lai, tru khi su kien bi huy boi ban to chuc.
- Co the chuyen nhuong ve cho nguoi khac truoc thoi gian check-in.

## Hoan ve
- Neu su kien bi huy, hoan tien se duoc xu ly trong 7 ngay lam viec.
- Lien he support@fevents.vn de yeu cau hoan ve.

## Check-in
- Check-in bang ma QR tren ve dien tu.
- Den dung gio, mang theo the sinh vien de xac thuc.

## Khac
- Moi thac mac vui long lien he support@fevents.vn hoac hoi chatbot nay.
